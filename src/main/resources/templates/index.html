How does it work?

Download your data file from the TikTok app and upload for analysis! Just follow these steps:

Step 1: Tap "Me" in the bottom right of the TikTok app
Step 2: Hit the 3 dots in the top right corner
Step 3: Click on "Privacy and safety"
Step 4: Tap "Personalization and data"
Step 5: Click to "Download your data"

After 1-2 days the data file will be able to be downloaded. Then, upload and analyze your data!
